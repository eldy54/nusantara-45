Welcome to Javaneese Batik

Batik Mega Mendung

The Mega Mendung Batik motif is an identical work of batik art and has even become an icon of batik in Cirebon and other parts of Indonesia. This batik motif has a uniqueness that is not found in other batik-producing areas.

Batik Singa Barong

The Singo Barong motif comes from the Djojo Koesoemo Batik Center in Blitar. This batik is a story that forms a pattern like a story with various kinds of advice. The advice contained in it is a benchmark for leadership.

Batik Sekar Jagad

Batik Sekar Jagad is one of the batik motifs originating from Solo and Yogyakarta. This motif contains the meaning of beauty and beauty so that other people who see will be fascinated.

Batik Pring Sedapur

The Pring Sedapur Batik motif has a very high philosophical meaning. Bamboo plants usually live in groups, forming a single force. Bamboo when united will become a strength, if unraveled into a very tight rope.
